Gaiablock

Welcome to Gaiablock

An opensource, decentralized network for scientifically verifiable health decision making, providing individuals with a new way to share and compare data from their wearable sensors in realtime.

Understanding

Science has given us the best understanding of nature and how our bodies work. Even if you know nothing about science, the knowledge available in the network is placed at your fingertips.

Knowledge

The health knowledge is intelligently applied to the data so that the best outcomes are attained.

Community

All are treated equally on the network. There are no barriers to contributions of either science or data. History shows us that new ideas and breakthroughs can come from any and all members of the community.

How it works

The sensors, data and science are combined in an intelligent collaboration across all members in the network. This starts with registering a wearable sensor and securing an account on the network.

Wearable sensor

The network automatically discovers wireless connections to sensors and seeks permission to include them.

Realtime feedback

Given the context of the wearable data, a 3D human simulates the understanding.

Control how your data is used

The more we share, the better we know, the results improve with accuracy as the community grows. The benefits gained from this are equally shared throughout all participants.

Better living

The network is always on, always working for you, adapting the science to match your needs.
